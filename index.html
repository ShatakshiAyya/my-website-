South Korea

South Korea is a vibrant country known for its modern cities, historic temples, and beautiful landscapes.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Seoul – The dynamic capital city.
  • Jeju Island – Stunning beaches and volcanic landscapes.
  • Gyeongju – Ancient capital with rich history.
  • Busan – Beaches, seafood, and mountains.

USA

The United States offers diverse experiences from bustling cities to breathtaking national parks.

Top Places to Visit:

  • New York City – Iconic landmarks and culture.
  • Grand Canyon – Natural wonder of the world.
  • San Francisco – Golden Gate Bridge and vibrant neighborhoods.
  • Yellowstone National Park – Wildlife and geysers.

Paris, France

Paris is the city of lights, famous for its art, fashion, and romantic ambiance.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Eiffel Tower – Iconic landmark.
  • Louvre Museum – World-class art collections.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral – Gothic architecture.
  • Montmartre – Artistic neighborhood with stunning views.

Japan

Japan combines ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology and natural beauty.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Tokyo – A bustling metropolis.
  • Kyoto – Historic temples and gardens.
  • Mount Fuji – Japan's tallest peak.
  • Osaka – Famous for street food and nightlife.
